linking people with food

Lofo is an application that provides travelers with information of local food and restaurants. Travelers can also build connections with locals by going out and having a meal together. This is my very first design project.

motivation

We, as human beings, all eat. Throughout history, the food that we eat had shaped civilizations and cultures. Thus, when traveling, eating local food can make the travelers' experiences better. So how would travelers, as strangers to a place, find places to eat?

Following the problem-solution model, Angela and I started identifying problems that contemporary travelers face when finding places to eat, by brainstorming on our own and through surveys and interviews with other people.

 

We came up with these general problems:

  • Gap between local residents and travelers
  • Lack of trustworthy restaurant finder
  • Lack of knowledge of local dishes
  • People's different needs from the app
  • Travelers want to try real home food

With the problems identified, Angela and I started the initial ideation of the app.

 

A stakeholder map was created by Angela, identifying target users of the app.
