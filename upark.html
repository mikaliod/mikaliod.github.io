UPark

parking made easy

UPark is an application that connects drivers with private driveway owners. The driveway owners can lend out their private driveways as parking spots, and drivers can make reservations for parking. With this application, interactions between the two groups are easy, quick and secure. This is a class project for Interaction Design Overview. Research was done by another group and the findings were handed to us.

1.

problem

The problem we identified from the research was two-fold:

 

  1. Driveway owners value security.
  2.  
  3. Homeowners and landlords want to utilize their unused vacant parking spaces, but are nervous about lending part of their home out to strangers.
  4.  
  5. Drivers value convenience.
  6.  
  7. Students, commuters, and tourists want to easily find parking on the go without having to worry about parking permits or expensive lots.

2.

ideation

Ideation of the application was done through creating persona, scenarios and storyboards. Four persona were created, including homeowner, student, tourist and businessman, and the ideation process aimed at solving their specific pain points. You can view details in the downloadable process book in the end.

3.

solution

The solution is UPark, a private driveway parking application. The driveway owners can lend out their private driveways without worrying about misbehaving drivers by looking at reviews and ratings of the drivers. The drivers can make reservations for parking easily by searching by location on a map view. We designed a mobile application to be used mainly by drivers, as it's portable and can be used on the car, and a desktop application to be used by the driveway owners, as the bigger display would be better for management.

  • Jessica
  • Tourist
  • New to the area
  • Is frustrated by all the permit-only parking spots

How would Jessica find this app helpful?

clear map view

The map view gives Jessica a clear idea about what parking spots are closest to her.

easy reservation

With just a few taps and slides, Jessica can make a reservation within seconds. It gives Jessica the ability to select available time slots, choose between recurring or one-time reservation, and provides her with pricing information and the reviews and ratings of the owner from other drivers.

informative reservation page

The reservation page presents all of the important information to Jessica in a clear fashion. Color coding makes it easy for her to see whether her reservations have been approved or not. There's also a section in which she can retrieve directions to her upcoming parking spots and review past parking experiences.

  • Bob
  • Homeowner in quiet neighborhood in Pittsburgh
  • Wants to rent out his parking space
  • Is concerned about safety and security in renting out to strangers

How would Bob find this app helpful?

approving requests

From the desktop application for driveway owners, Bob can see the reviews of Jessica from other driveway owners. This ensures Bob that Jessica can be a responsible and behaving renter, and alleviate Bob's worries.
