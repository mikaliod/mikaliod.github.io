1.

problem

The problem we identified from the research was two-fold:

 

  1. Driveway owners value security.
  2.  
  3. Homeowners and landlords want to utilize their unused vacant parking spaces, but are nervous about lending part of their home out to strangers.
  4.  
  5. Drivers value convenience.
  6.  
  7. Students, commuters, and tourists want to easily find parking on the go without having to worry about parking permits or expensive lots.

2.

ideation

Ideation of the application was done through creating persona, scenarios and storyboards. Four persona were created, including homeowner, student, tourist and businessman, and the ideation process aimed at solving their specific pain points. You can view details in the downloadable process book in the end.

3.

solution

The solution is UPark, a private driveway parking application. The driveway owners can lend out their private driveways without worrying about misbehaving drivers by looking at reviews and ratings of the drivers. The drivers can make reservations for parking easily by searching by location on a map view. We designed a mobile application to be used mainly by drivers, as it's portable and can be used on the car, and a desktop application to be used by the driveway owners, as the bigger display would be better for management.
